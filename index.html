Why Choose BARKATZ

Craftsmanship that speaks to the soul

Master Craftsmanship

Each piece is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional techniques passed down through generations, ensuring exceptional quality and authenticity.

Premium Materials

We source only the finest materials - sterling silver, precious metals, and authentic Jerusalem olive wood - to create heirloom pieces that last for generations.

Sacred Tradition

Our designs honor centuries of Jewish tradition while incorporating contemporary elegance, creating pieces that are both spiritually meaningful and aesthetically beautiful.

The Artisan

Master craftsman Esti Barkatz has dedicated over three decades to the art of creating sacred Judaica. Born in Jerusalem and trained by the finest artisans in the Old City, Esti combines ancient techniques with contemporary design sensibilities.

Her workshop, nestled in the heart of the Jewish Quarter, serves as both a creative sanctuary and a bridge between tradition and innovation. Each piece that emerges from her hands carries the weight of history and the promise of future generations.

The Philosophy

For Esti, creating Judaica is not merely a craft—it's a spiritual journey. "Every curve, every engraving, every polish" reflects her deep understanding of Jewish tradition and her commitment to preserving its beauty for posterity.

Her philosophy centers on the belief that sacred objects should elevate the ordinary into the extraordinary, transforming everyday rituals into moments of profound spiritual connection. This vision infuses every piece with soul and meaning.
